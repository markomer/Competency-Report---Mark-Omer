Biography

- a little bit about myself -

My name is Mark Omer, born through mostly Swedish ancestry - three of my grandparents were Swedish natives. I am very proud to be an American, but I am also very proud of who my forebears were and where they came from from whence they came.

Throughout my military career, which spanned over 27 years, I mostly worked as a linguist. I learned Japanese, Polish and Mandarin Chinese though rarely used these languages directly. Since I retired from the military I have been an artist and art teacher. I have taught in my studio, at a high school and at a community college. I am very excited for this current opportunity to learn about software development, and hope to implement this new skill into freelance work.
